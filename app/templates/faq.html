Frequently Asked Questions

What in the world is this website?

This site is for players of the Jewel-Osco Monopoly game. If you're not sure what that is (or not even sure what Jewel-Osco is), I honestly feel a little bad for you because it's amazeballs.

For those that are playing, use this site to post and exchange tickets with your fellow gamers.

I have a lot of duplicate tickets. Should I enter them all?

If you see that your duplicate hasn't been entered yet, yes! But if there are a lot of different entries for the same ticket, it might not be helpful to enter another one.

I've found a ticket that I'd like to claim. How do I do that?

The ticket owner's Facebook page will be linked next to the ticket. Send them a message through Facebook to request the ticket.

I think I found a website bug. Where can I report it?

Great question! All bugs should be entered into the issues tracker.

I can't seem to login on my mobile phone with Chrome?

This is a known issue and a big bummer. Until I'm able to find a workaround, try using another web browser on your mobile device or use your desktop computer instead.
