Frequently Asked Questions

What in the world is this website?

This site is for players of the 2016 grocery store Monopoly game. If you're not sure what that is, don't feel bad. It might not be available in your area.

For those that are playing, use this site to post and exchange tickets with your fellow gamers.

Can I use this website for my grocery store's Monopoly game?

The following stores are listed on the Monopoly board that this website was designed to support:

Vons, Pavilions, Amigos, Albertsons, Safeway, Acme, Jewel-Osco, Shaws, Albertsons, Carrs, Market Street, United Supermarkets, United Express, Randalls, Tom Thumb, Star Market

I have a lot of duplicate tickets. Should I enter them all?

If you see that your duplicate hasn't been entered yet, yes! But if there are a lot of different entries for the same ticket, it might not be helpful to enter another one.

I've found a ticket that I'd like to claim. How do I do that?

The ticket owner's Facebook page will be linked next to the ticket. Send them a message through Facebook to request the ticket.

How will I know when a ticket I need becomes available?

First, make sure that you mark the ticket that you need as "Needed" when you submit it. If another user has entered your ticket as "Available", you'll receive an email alert notifying you. Make sure that you've entered your email address in your account page.

When you're notified of a match, come back to the directory, locate the ticket that's available, and contact that user through the Facebook profile they've provided.

Why should I enter my email address in the account page?

The directory will automatically send you an email when the tickets you need become available. You're not required to enter your email address though and your email will never be made available to the public. You can remove it at any time.

Will you add other ways to login besides Facebook?

Honestly, it doesn't look like that'll happen this time around. This was the best solution to allow for users to connect with each other quickly and it keeps the amount of personal details you need to enter to a bare minimum. It couldn't get any easier.

I think I found a website bug. Where can I report it?

Great question! All bugs should be entered into the issues tracker.
